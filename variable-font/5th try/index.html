CHOREO is a variable display typeface designed by Dan Luo.



It was initially inspired by a cover of JUGEND MAGAZINE, where two girls in white dresses hold their hands and dance by the water.

The six letters on the top are perfectly in tune with the scene as if dancing on tiptoes with dresses billowing around, moving their bodies tirelessly, never falling on the ground.





The letters' exaggerated teardrop terminals were inspired by DANCE NOTATIONS, which combine music notes and more expressive graphic symbols to represent body movements.
