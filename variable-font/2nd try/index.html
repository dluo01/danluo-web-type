Choreo is a variable display font designed by Dan Luo.


It was initially inspired by a cover of Jugend magazine, where two girls in white dresses hold their hands and dance by the water.


Its exaggerated teardrop terminals were inspired by dance notations, which combine music notes and more expressive graphic symbols to represent body movements.
